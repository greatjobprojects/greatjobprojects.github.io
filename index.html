About

Geoffrey Byers

Geoffrey is volatile mix of a thinker and a doer. He obsessively designs, builds, hacks and hustles. Prior to founding Great Job, Geoffrey worked for NASA on the external tank for the space shuttle and with Siemens designing products for the medical, building technology, and automation industries. Geoffrey is an avid swimmer, jazz musician, and semi-professional diesel mechanic.

Jordan Braun

Jordan is a designer with a focus on abstract problem solving. He is a founder and designer at Great Job. Prior to founding Great Job, Jordan worked as both a UX Designer and Product Manager at two Chicago-based startups. He loves to push his own boundaries and is often experimenting with diet, exercise, and lifestyle.
